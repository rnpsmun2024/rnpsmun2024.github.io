International Press Corps

The international press corps, better known as IPC, is the place to be this RNPS MUN. Known for its relentless pursuit of the truth and a love for the democratic process, delegates will partake in a ruthless debate to end all debates. Defending their journalistic integrity and ethical superiority, one must prove their prowess to earn their spot in committee. Would an independent form of authority improve accountability? Or is it the sworn duty of the government to oversee media proceedings?

Together, let us join hands to protect the rights of man and ensure justice above all.
