WHAT IS
R-NPSMUN?

NPS Rajajinagar proudly presents the eighth edition of R-NPSMUN!

An amalgamation of consistent committees fused with a few unconventional ones, we have ensured that each delegate, new or experienced, has something to look forward to. Delegates will have to think on their feet to keep up with the pace of the debate, combating the obstacles posed, while sticking to the portfolios represented.

Alliances will be made, strategies devised, while keeping the nations’ best interests at heart. Factual knowledge along with rational arbitrations will help sail you through! So delegates, come be a part of this exhilarating experience!

Secretary General of RNPSMUN 2024

Mansi Hiremath


As a passionate leader carrying a contagious smile, she commands respect effortlessly. Her fervour drives her relentless pursuit of excellence, evident in her tireless work ethic which her friends like to call ‘concerning sleep schedule’. Exceptional problem solving skills allow her to navigate through any challenge thrown at her. Her charismatic personality ensures that her leadership style has a little flair to it. Gracefully wearing her heart on her sleeve, she approaches those in need with a kind smile and a spirit that’s all set to do everything to make them feel better. Striving to be nothing but the best, she holds herself at high standards that she isn’t afraid to reach.

This is Mansi Hiremath, Secretary General of R-NPS MUN’24: The Eighth Edition.

    LETTER FROM THE SECRETARY GENERAL

    Dear Delegates,

        As Secretary-General of R-NPSMUN 2024: The 7th Edition, I am thrilled to extend my warmest greetings to each and every one of you. Going from a shy 8th grader to being the Sec Gen of a MUN has been one of the most fulfilling and validating experiences of my life. That is what I hope MUN will become for you, it is more than fiery debates and intensive research, it is the ability to make lifelong connections and unforgettable memories. Whether it is your first time attending a MUN or your tenth, I promise R-NPSMUN 2024 will be one to remember, our top-notch Executive Board and dedicated Secretariat have done everything they can to ensure a smooth conference for you all. So I urge you, my dear delegates, to seize this opportunity, immerse yourselves in the world of diplomacy, and leave no stone unturned in your pursuit of excellence. I have immense faith in your ability to take R-NPSMUN to its pinnacle.

    Get ready to join the madness, embrace the chaos, and let your inner diplomat shine. Remember, it's not just about winning awards and accolades. It's about showcasing your eloquence while managing to keep a straight face and mastering the art of diplomatic charm with a smile. Please feel free to reach out to me for anything, before or during the conference.

    May the odds be ever in your favour!

    -Mansi Hiremath
