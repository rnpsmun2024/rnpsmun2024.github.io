Lok Sabha

Navigating this crisis committee, your mandate transcends mere deliberation; it demands decisive action and transformative leadership. As delegates, your collective responsibility lies in forging a path forward from the wreckage of past mistakes in the National Education Policy (NEP). Your goal must be clear and resolute: to draft an NEP that not only rectifies past negligence but also sets a new standard of excellence and integrity. You as delegates stand at a critical juncture where every decision holds the potential to restore faith in our educational institutions and safeguard the future of millions of students. Seize this opportunity to redefine educational governance, ensuring that the policies reflect the highest standards of fairness and efficiency.

Together, you as delegates should emerge from this crisis committee not only with a revised NEP but with a blueprint for a resilient and accountable educational framework that meets the aspirations of our nation's youth.
